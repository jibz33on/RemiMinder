RemiMinder

AI-powered visit intelligence — records doctor appointments, extracts a structured clinical record, and keeps patients and caregivers informed.

Flutter Mobile FastAPI · Cloud Run Google STT Gemini · Vertex AI Firebase Auth
The Problem

Patients forget most of what doctors say

80%
of medical information forgotten immediately after a visit
50%
of what is retained is remembered incorrectly
40%
of patients don't follow treatment plans due to confusion

Caregivers have no visibility. Medications are missed. Follow-up actions are never taken. The information gap between the clinic and the home is the biggest failure point in chronic care.

The Solution

Record once. Understand everything.

🎙️

Record

Patient taps record in the Flutter app. Audio is captured and uploaded to Google Cloud Storage.

🧠

Extract

Google STT transcribes the audio. Gemini extracts a validated clinical JSON record.

📋

Deliver

Reminders, tasks, and caregiver alerts are generated automatically from the structured record.

The patient keeps a searchable, structured account of every visit. The caregiver gets real-time visibility into diagnoses, medications, and follow-up actions — without accessing raw audio.

STT-2 Design

Three guarantees
patients rely on

🎯
Consistent
Same transcript always produces the same output — patients can trust the data won't change between runs.
Gemini at temperature = 0.0 · no randomness
✅
Validated
Only explicitly stated facts are extracted — no inference, no hallucinated fields. Missing or extra keys are rejected.
9-field schema · field-by-field check · auto JSON repair · retry on failure
📋
Auditable
Every extraction is traceable end-to-end — essential for clinical accountability and model versioning.
Logs: model · prompt version · latency · token count · cost

Raw Input

What the microphone picks up

RECORDING IN PROGRESS 08:24
Doctor
So your HbA1c came back at 8.4 this time. Last visit it was 7.1 — that's gone up more than I'd like. How have you been taking the metformin?
Patient
Most days yeah, but I've been skipping the evening one sometimes. Work's been stressful and I just forget.
Doctor
Okay. I'm going to increase the metformin to 1000mg twice daily — start that tomorrow. I also want to add semaglutide, 0.25mg weekly injection. We'll titrate to 0.5 after four weeks if you tolerate it.
Patient
Is that an injection? Will it cause nausea? I've heard people have trouble with it.
Doctor
Some nausea is common early on, yes. Start slow with meals. Your blood pressure also came in at 138 over 88 today — I want you to cut sodium down, under 2 grams a day. Let's see if lifestyle gets it down before we add anything. Come back in 12 weeks and we'll recheck the HbA1c and BP.
This raw audio is uploaded to GCS and fed into the two-stage extraction pipeline →

Downstream Intelligence

From one JSON record to patient action

⏰
Medication Reminders
Parsed from the medications field. Scheduled reminders with dosage and frequency, delivered via FCM push.
✅
Follow-up Tasks
Generated from action_items. Trackable tasks the patient can complete or dismiss in the app.
👨‍👩‍👧
Caregiver Alerts
Role-scoped read-only view. Caregivers see diagnoses, medications, and tasks — never raw audio or transcript text.
All downstream processing runs from process_downstream_for_visit() — a single function that receives the validated JSON and fans out to reminder generation, task creation, and caregiver notification in parallel.
